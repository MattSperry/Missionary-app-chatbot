Ask for Help

Reach out to someone who cares. You are not alone.

If you're struggling and need someone to talk to, don't hesitate to reach out. You can call a helpline, talk to a friend, or seek professional help.

Helpline Numbers

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline: 988

Crisis Text Line: Text "HELLO" to 741741
